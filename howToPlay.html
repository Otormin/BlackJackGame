How to play

🎯 Objective:
Try to get the sum of your cards as close to 21 as possible without going over. If you hit exactly 21, you win Blackjack!

Step 1: Enter Your Bet
- Enter an amount of chips you'd like to bet in the input field.
- The minimum bet is 10 chips.
- You cannot bet more chips than you currently have.

▶️ Step 2: Start the Game
- Click "Start Game" to draw your first two cards.
- The sum of the two cards will be displayed.

🔄 Step 3: Choose Your Move
- If the sum is:
    • Less than 21: You can draw a new card by clicking "New Card".
    • Exactly 21: 🎉 Blackjack! You win double your bet.
    • Over 21: 😞 You lose the round and your bet is deducted.

💰 Winning & Losing Chips
- Win (Blackjack): You earn double the chips you bet.
- Lose (Over 21): You lose the amount you bet.
- Continue (Below 21): You can draw new cards.

🔄 Game Over
- If your total chips fall below 10, the game ends.
- Reload or reset the game to start over.
